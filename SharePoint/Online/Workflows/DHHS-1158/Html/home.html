Welcome to the Accounts Receivable Site!


The Accounts Receivable site is currently used to maintain the Accounts Receivable Certification (1158) Form. The Accounts Receivable department is responsible for billing and collecting Medicaid overpayments from providers and recipients. The 1158 form is prepared by various program areas when Medicaid overpayments have been identified. This site may contain data related to Personal Private Information or PPI.
